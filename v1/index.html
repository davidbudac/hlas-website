Your voice is the fastest keyboard you own.

Hlas (Slovak for "voice") turns speech into text instantly -- right where your cursor sits. No switching apps, no copy-pasting. It runs on-device with WhisperKit for total privacy, or routes to a remote GPU server when you need maximum speed. Auto-detects your language, strips filler words, adds smart punctuation. Works in every app on your Mac.

Features

What it does.

01

Local AI Engine

On-device transcription powered by WhisperKit. Your audio never leaves your Mac. No internet required. Total privacy by default.

02

Remote GPU Server

Route to a remote GPU for faster-than-real-time transcription on longer recordings. Switch between local and remote with one click.

03

60+ Languages

Automatic language detection across 60+ languages. Switch mid-sentence if you want. Whisper handles it.

04

Smart Text Processing

Filler word removal ("um," "uh," "like"), smart punctuation, capitalization, and a personal dictionary for names and jargon.

05

Translation

Built-in translation via Apple Translation Framework and Whisper's native translation. Speak in one language, type in another.

06

Works Everywhere

Text is inserted wherever your cursor sits -- browsers, code editors, email clients, Slack, Terminal, Notes. If you can type there, Hlas works there.
