// transcription engines

Two engines. Your choice.

Privacy-first local processing or lightning-fast cloud. Switch anytime.

Local

On-Device AI

Runs entirely on your Mac using WhisperKit. Your audio never leaves the machine. Complete privacy, zero latency dependency.

  • WhisperKit / Whisper models
  • Apple Neural Engine optimized
  • No internet required
  • 100% private -- audio stays local
  • Multiple model sizes available
Remote

GPU Server

Offload to a remote GPU for maximum accuracy on longer dictations. Ideal when you want the largest models without local compute cost.

  • Remote GPU acceleration
  • Largest model support
  • Ideal for long-form dictation
  • Low-latency streaming
  • Automatic fallback to local
