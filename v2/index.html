Transcription

Two engines. Your choice.

Local

On-Device AI

WhisperKit runs Whisper models directly on your Mac. Your audio never leaves the machine. Complete privacy, zero latency dependency.

  • Runs on Apple Neural Engine & GPU
  • No internet required
  • Full data privacy
  • Multiple model sizes available
  • Apple Silicon optimized
Remote

GPU Server

Send audio to a remote GPU server running larger Whisper models. Higher accuracy for complex dictation, longer recordings, and noisier environments.

  • Larger models, higher accuracy
  • Offloads compute from your Mac
  • Handles long-form audio
  • Best for noisy environments
  • Configurable server endpoint
